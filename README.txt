GiftBerry

Welcome to GiftBerry!

GiftBerry is a social-media website designed to simplify the gift buying and receiving process, cutting out the stress of remembering
birthdays, guesswork around what to buy a friend, or struggle to find gifts our loves ones will cherish without breaking the bank. 
GiftBerry allows users to tell others what kinds of gifts they're interested in receiving, and provides shoppers with an array of 
handy search options and gift buying aids.

Enjoy!
